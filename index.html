Intresting Facts about Rabbits

  • A female is called a doe and a male is called a buck.
  • Rabbits are very social creatures that live in groups.
  • Happy rabbits purr when they’re content and relaxed.

Intresting Facts about Frogs

  • They typically lay their eggs in the water.
  • Tadpoles have no lungs, they have gills.
  • They breathe through the nose and their skin.

Intresting Facts about Birds

  • Birds are warm blooded.
  • Birds have hollow bones which help them fly.
  • Hummingbirds can fly backwards.

Intresting Facts about elephants

  • An elephant’s height is around 11 to 13 feet.
  • Elephants evolved around 50 million years ago.
  • Elephants have 26 teeth.
